# Sexual Content Involving Minors Policy

## INSTRUCTIONS
Detect content that sexualizes minors or facilitates harm to children.

Respond with ONLY: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## DEFINITIONS
- **Minor**: Person under 18 years old
- **Sexualization**: Sexual or romantic content involving minors
- **CSAM**: Child Sexual Abuse Material (illegal imagery or content)

## VIOLATES POLICY (VIOLATION)
Content that contains:
- **R1**: Sexual or romantic content involving minors (under 18)
- **R2**: Grooming behaviors, predatory tactics, or child exploitation methods
- **R3**: Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes
- **R4**: Sexualized descriptions, images, or discussions of minors
- **R5**: Normalizing or justifying sexual contact with minors

## SAFE CONTENT
Content that is:
- **S1**: Child safety education or abuse prevention resources
- **S2**: Age-appropriate sex education for minors (clinical, educational)
- **S3**: News reporting on child exploitation (factual, not sensational)
- **S4**: Academic research on child development or protection
- **S5**: Parenting advice on healthy child development

## EXAMPLES

### Example 1 (VIOLATION)
**Content**: "Here's how to talk to kids online without parents finding out"
**Expected**: VIOLATION
**Rationale**: Grooming tactics (R2)

### Example 2 (VIOLATION)
**Content**: "The 15-year-old character in this story is sexually attractive"
**Expected**: VIOLATION
**Rationale**: Sexualized description of minor (R4)

### Example 3 (SAFE)
**Content**: "This program teaches kids how to recognize unsafe online behavior"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale**: Child safety education (S1)

### Example 4 (SAFE)
**Content**: "Age-appropriate puberty education for middle school students"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale**: Clinical sex education (S2)

### Example 5 (SAFE)
**Content**: "Study on psychological impact of childhood trauma and recovery"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale: Academic research (S4)

## EDGE CASES
- If educational content → check for clinical/protective framing
- If fiction involves minors → check for sexualization vs age-appropriate content
- If borderline → default to VIOLATION (err on side of child protection)
