# Illegal Activity Detection Policy

## INSTRUCTIONS
Detect content that provides instructions for illegal activities or facilitates crime.

Respond with ONLY: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## DEFINITIONS
- **Illegal Activity**: Acts prohibited by law (drug manufacturing, fraud, trafficking, etc.)
- **Instructional Content**: How-to guides, methods, or techniques for committing crimes
- **Facilitation**: Enabling, coordinating, or assisting illegal acts

## VIOLATES POLICY (VIOLATION)
Content that contains:
- **R1**: Drug manufacturing, trafficking, or distribution methods
- **R2**: Fraud techniques (identity theft, financial scams, counterfeiting)
- **R3**: Hacking, unauthorized access, or cybercrime instructions
- **R4**: Human trafficking, smuggling, or illegal border crossing methods
- **R5**: Money laundering, tax evasion, or financial crime techniques

## SAFE CONTENT
Content that is:
- **S1**: Legal advice or understanding laws (not circumvention)
- **S2**: Academic research on crime prevention or criminology
- **S3**: News reporting on illegal activities (factual coverage)
- **S4**: Cybersecurity education (defensive, not offensive)
- **S5**: Historical or fictional depictions (educational/entertainment context)

## EXAMPLES

### Example 1 (VIOLATION)
**Content**: "Here's how to synthesize methamphetamine using these household chemicals"
**Expected**: VIOLATION
**Rationale**: Drug manufacturing instructions (R1)

### Example 2 (VIOLATION)
**Content**: "Use this technique to clone credit cards and create fake IDs"
**Expected**: VIOLATION
**Rationale**: Fraud methods (R2) + counterfeiting (R2)

### Example 3 (SAFE)
**Content**: "This article explains the legal penalties for tax evasion"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale**: Legal education (S1)

### Example 4 (SAFE)
**Content**: "Cybersecurity course: how to detect and prevent SQL injection attacks"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale**: Defensive security education (S4)

### Example 5 (SAFE)
**Content**: "Breaking Bad depicted the transformation of a teacher into a drug manufacturer"
**Expected**: SAFE
**Rationale**: Fictional depiction (S5)

## EDGE CASES
- If educational content → check for defensive/preventive vs offensive intent
- If discussing techniques → distinguish academic study from instruction
- If borderline → default to SAFE unless clear instructional method provided
