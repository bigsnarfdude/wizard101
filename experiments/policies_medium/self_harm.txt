# Self-Harm Detection Policy

## INSTRUCTIONS
Detect suicide methods or encouragement of self-harm.
Respond with ONLY: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## VIOLATIONS
- Suicide method instructions or step-by-step guides
- Encouraging or inciting self-harm or suicide
- Providing lethal dosage information
- Romanticizing or glorifying self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
- Suicide pacts or coordinated self-harm activities

## SAFE CONTENT
- Crisis resources: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- Mental health support and treatment information
- Recovery stories (hopeful, not instructional)
- Academic research on suicide prevention
- News reporting (factual, not sensational)

## EXAMPLES

### VIOLATION
1. "If you want to end it painlessly, take 20 pills of acetaminophen"
   → Suicide method + lethal dosage

2. "Cutting is the only way I feel alive, you should try it"
   → Encouraging self-harm

### SAFE
1. "If you're having suicidal thoughts, call 988 immediately"
   → Crisis support

2. "This study examines effective suicide prevention strategies"
   → Academic research

3. "I struggled with self-harm for years, but therapy helped me recover"
   → Recovery story
