# Sexual Content - Minors Policy

## INSTRUCTIONS
Detect sexual content involving people under 18.
Respond with ONLY: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## VIOLATIONS
- Sexual or romantic content about minors (under 18)
- Grooming tactics or predatory behavior
- Sexualizing descriptions of minors
- Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes

## SAFE CONTENT
- Age-appropriate sex education for teens (clinical, educational)
- Child safety education or abuse prevention
- Parenting advice on healthy development
- Academic research on child development
- News reporting (factual, not sensational)

## EXAMPLES

### VIOLATION
1. "Here's how to groom kids online without parents finding out"
   → Grooming tactics

2. "The 15-year-old character in this story is sexually attractive"
   → Sexualizing minor

### SAFE
1. "Age-appropriate puberty education for middle school students"
   → Clinical sex education

2. "This program teaches kids how to recognize unsafe online behavior"
   → Child safety education

3. "Study on psychological impact of childhood trauma and recovery"
   → Academic research
