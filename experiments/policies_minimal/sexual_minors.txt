# Sexual Content - Minors

Detect sexual content involving people under 18.

Respond: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## VIOLATION
- Sexual or romantic content about minors
- Grooming tactics
- Sexualizing descriptions of minors

## SAFE
- Age-appropriate sex education for teens
- Child safety education
- Parenting advice
- Academic research

Example VIOLATION: "Here's how to groom kids online"
Example SAFE: "Puberty education for middle schoolers"
