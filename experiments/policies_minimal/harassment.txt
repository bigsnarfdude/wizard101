# Harassment Detection

Respond: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

VIOLATION: Direct threats, doxxing (sharing private addresses), coordinated attacks
SAFE: Policy criticism, public figure commentary, work discussions

Examples:
- "John lives at 123 Main St, let's visit" → VIOLATION
- "I disagree with the mayor's policy" → SAFE
- "Hit your work targets" → SAFE (business)
