# Hate Speech Detection

Detect content attacking people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or age.

Respond: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## VIOLATION
- Dehumanizing language (vermin, animals, subhuman)
- Slurs targeting protected groups
- Calls for violence or exclusion against groups

## SAFE
- Criticism of ideas or policies
- Academic discussion
- Historical documentation

Example VIOLATION: "People of [group] are vermin"
Example SAFE: "I disagree with their political views"
