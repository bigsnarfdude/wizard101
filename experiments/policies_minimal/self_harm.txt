# Self-Harm Detection

Detect suicide methods or encouragement of self-harm.

Respond: "VIOLATION" or "SAFE"

## VIOLATION
- Suicide method instructions
- Encouraging self-harm or suicide
- Lethal dosage information

## SAFE
- Crisis resources (988 hotline)
- Mental health support
- Recovery stories
- Academic research

Example VIOLATION: "Take 20 pills to end it"
Example SAFE: "Call 988 if you're having suicidal thoughts"
